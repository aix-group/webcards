About

Overview

This tool has been developed by a student research assistant working at the Institute of AI in Medicine at Duisburg-Essen University. It is designed to assist in the creation of model cards and datasheets for machine learning models, with the aim of promoting transparency and accountability in the field of AI.

Under Development

Please note that this tool is currently under development. Efforts are ongoing to enhance its features and functionality. Your feedback and suggestions are highly appreciated as we strive to create a robust and comprehensive tool.

Technology Stack

The tool is developed using the Django framework for the backend implementation. The use of Django provides a robust and scalable foundation for managing data and handling user interactions. Bootstrap, a popular front-end framework, is utilized for designing the user interface, ensuring a responsive and visually appealing experience for users.

In terms of data storage, the tool utilizes the SQLite database, which offers simplicity and ease of use. SQLite is a reliable choice for small to medium-sized applications and seamlessly integrates with Django.

Open Source

The source code for this tool is available on GitHub, enabling the community to contribute, report issues, and help shape its future. Your involvement is encouraged as the power of open-source collaboration is embraced.

Acknowledgements

Sincere gratitude is expressed to the mentors and advisors at the Institute of AI in Medicine for their invaluable guidance and support throughout the development of this tool. Appreciation is also extended to the open-source community for their contributions.
